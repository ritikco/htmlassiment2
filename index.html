about my city

lal-Kuan has excellent connectivity to other parts of the city via the GT Road, Delhi-Meerut Expressway and Cres Park Road. The Shaheed Sthal New Bus Adda and the Noida Electronic City Metro Station (Blue Line) are situated at 8Km via National Highway-34 and 12Km via Delhi - Meerut Expressway respectively. Further, the Ghaziabad Junction is 10Km and Ghaziabad Domestic Airport is 23Km, both can be reached via Delhi - Meerut Expressway. Nestled in the eastern part of Ghaziabad, Lal Kuan is a developing locality. Proximity to Business parks, excellent connectivity to other parts of the city are the key factors for the residential growth. Modern Railway City, Shahpur Bamheta and Pandit Colony are some of the neighboring localities. Sare Springview Floors and Urban Homes Aditya World City are some of the well-known residential projects in Lal Kuan.
